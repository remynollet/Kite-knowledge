# Technical Glossary

Definitions for technical terms used throughout Kite-knowledge.

### Bridle
A system of lines connecting the kite to the flying line, used to control the angle of attack.
*(FR: bridage, DE: Waage, IT: briglia, ES: brida)*

### Leading edge
The front edge of the sail that meets the wind first.
*(FR: bord d'attaque, DE: Leitkante, IT: bordo d'attacco, ES: borde de ataque)*

### Lift
The aerodynamic force that allows the kite to rise and stay aloft.
*(FR: portance, DE: Auftrieb, IT: portanza, ES: sustentación)*

### Sail
The flexible part of the kite that captures the wind to generate lift.
*(FR: voile, DE: Segel, IT: vela, ES: vela)*

### Spar
A rigid element of the frame (made of carbon, fiberglass, or wood) that keeps the sail taut.
*(FR: vergue, DE: Spreize, IT: traversa, ES: travesaño)*

### Spine
The central element of the frame running vertically along the axis of symmetry.
*(FR: quille, DE: Kiel, IT: chiglia, ES: quilla)*

### Trailing edge
The rear edge of the sail where the wind flows off.
*(FR: bord de fuite, DE: Schleppkante, IT: bordo d'uscita, ES: borde de fuga)*
