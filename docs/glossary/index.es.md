# Glosario Técnico

Definiciones de términos técnicos utilizados en Kite-knowledge.

### Ajuste de brida
Changing the length of bridle lines to adapt the flight to wind conditions.
*(FR: réglage de bridage, EN: bridle adjustment, DE: Waageeinstellung, IT: regolazione della briglia)*

### Borde de ataque
The front edge of the sail that meets the wind first.
*(FR: bord d'attaque, EN: leading edge, DE: Leitkante, IT: bordo d'attacco)*

### Borde de fuga
The rear edge of the sail where the wind flows off.
*(FR: bord de fuite, EN: trailing edge, DE: Schleppkante, IT: bordo d'uscita)*

### Brida
A system of lines connecting the kite to the flying line, used to control the angle of attack.
*(FR: bridage, EN: bridle, DE: Waage, IT: briglia)*

### Envergadura
The distance from the tip of one wing to the tip of the other.
*(FR: envergure, EN: wingspan, DE: Spannweite, IT: apertura alare)*

### Quilla
The central element of the frame running vertically along the axis of symmetry.
*(FR: quille, EN: spine, DE: Kiel, IT: chiglia)*

### Sustentación
The aerodynamic force that allows the kite to rise and stay aloft.
*(FR: portance, EN: lift, DE: Auftrieb, IT: portanza)*

### Travesaño
A rigid element of the frame (made of carbon, fiberglass, or wood) that keeps the sail taut.
*(FR: vergue, EN: spar, DE: Spreize, IT: traversa)*

### Vela
The flexible part of the kite that captures the wind to generate lift.
*(FR: voile, EN: sail, DE: Segel, IT: vela)*

### Ventilado
A kite model with openings (vents) in the sail to reduce pressure in high winds.
*(FR: ventilé, EN: vented, DE: ventiliert, IT: ventilato)*
