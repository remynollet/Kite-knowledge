# Technisches Glossar

Definitionen technischer Begriffe, die in Kite-knowledge verwendet werden.

### Auftrieb
The aerodynamic force that allows the kite to rise and stay aloft.
*(FR: portance, EN: lift, IT: portanza, ES: sustentación)*

### Kiel
The central element of the frame running vertically along the axis of symmetry.
*(FR: quille, EN: spine, IT: chiglia, ES: quilla)*

### Leitkante
The front edge of the sail that meets the wind first.
*(FR: bord d'attaque, EN: leading edge, IT: bordo d'attacco, ES: borde de ataque)*

### Schleppkante
The rear edge of the sail where the wind flows off.
*(FR: bord de fuite, EN: trailing edge, IT: bordo d'uscita, ES: borde de fuga)*

### Segel
The flexible part of the kite that captures the wind to generate lift.
*(FR: voile, EN: sail, IT: vela, ES: vela)*

### Spannweite
The distance from the tip of one wing to the tip of the other.
*(FR: envergure, EN: wingspan, IT: apertura alare, ES: envergadura)*

### Spreize
A rigid element of the frame (made of carbon, fiberglass, or wood) that keeps the sail taut.
*(FR: vergue, EN: spar, IT: traversa, ES: travesaño)*

### Ventiliert
A kite model with openings (vents) in the sail to reduce pressure in high winds.
*(FR: ventilé, EN: vented, IT: ventilato, ES: ventilado)*

### Waage
A system of lines connecting the kite to the flying line, used to control the angle of attack.
*(FR: bridage, EN: bridle, IT: briglia, ES: brida)*

### Waageeinstellung
Changing the length of bridle lines to adapt the flight to wind conditions.
*(FR: réglage de bridage, EN: bridle adjustment, IT: regolazione della briglia, ES: ajuste de brida)*
