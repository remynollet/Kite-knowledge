## How to use

- **Zoom:** Use your mouse wheel or pinch to zoom in and out.
- **Pan:** Click and drag the background to move the map.
- **Move Nodes:** Click and drag a node to pin it in a new position.
- **Navigate:** Click a node once to jump to the full documentation page for that entity.
